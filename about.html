About

int Lab, LLC
2020 — present

Int Lab is the leading information security research and development laboratory in Palawan. Founded in the year 2020 by security researcher Ignas Laude with the idea of developing higher sense of information security in Palawan and wider.

From its existence, Int Lab has found vulnerabilities and weaknesses across Philippines software companies and a large number of world famous software packages and web applications which are widely used for accomplishing everyday computer activities.

Int Lab has helped many organizations and companies for improving their information security including software products, web sites and internal networks for better and more secured online or offline experience.
